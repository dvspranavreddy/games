How to Play

Tic Tac Toe is a turn-based strategy game. The objective is to be the first to get a complete row, column, or diagonal of your symbol (X or O) on the grid.

  • Select your symbol and difficulty level.
  • Click on any empty cell to place your symbol.
  • The computer will automatically make its move.
  • Win by aligning 3 (or 4 if 4x4 grid) of your symbols in a row.
